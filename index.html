About Me

I'm a Colombian Digital Media Designer who has had the privilege of living and working in Canada. My passion for web design originated in my hometown, Medellín, where I grew up in a vibrant and diverse environment. The transition to Canada opened new doors and challenges in my professional life. I saw it as an opportunity to broaden my horizons and bring my unique design perspective to a new audience.

My design philosophy revolves around seamlessly merging aesthetics and functionality, ensuring that every website I create not only looks stunning but also provides an exceptional user experience.
I, Estefania Aguilar, am a testament to the beauty of embracing new horizons while staying true to my creative roots.
